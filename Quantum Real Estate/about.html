About Us

Founded by Nikhileshwar Shrirsagar in Amravati, QuantumSoft Technologies began with a vision & mission to help people find more than just a house — a true home. With personalized service, deep market understanding, and trusted client relationships, we make real estate transparent, approachable, and tailored to your future.
